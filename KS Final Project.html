My Trip to Europe

As a girl who grew up traveling the world with almost no limits, I have always had a desire to see all that the world has to offer. My father was born in Europe, so he has a large amount of family members that still live in his home country of Czech Republic. Due to his love for his home, we took a trip to that region of Europe every summer. While it was always fun to tour Prague and visit the surrounding countries with my family, I have never had the chance to go to some of the countries that I dream of visiting. Luckily, I have the opportunity to take a 12-day trip to Europe that I get to plan with my best friend this coming summer.
